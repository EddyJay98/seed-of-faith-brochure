ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Seed of Faith Montessori School is a faith-based institution committed to raising disciplined, confident and academically excellent learners.

Our school combines modern teaching methods with strong moral values, innovation and practical learning to prepare students for the future.

We provide a safe and nurturing environment where every child is encouraged to grow spiritually, socially and academically.
